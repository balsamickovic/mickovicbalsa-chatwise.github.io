Dear guests, welcome to Bled Rose hotel, where we put your well-being and satisfaction first, and at the same time offer you the highest quality services and try to satisfy all your wishes.

Bled Rose is a boutique designer hotel that impresses with its aesthetically perfect ambience, quality materials and playful range of colours that you simply must visit. With a unique location, aesthetically perfect ambience with unique interior, modern conference rooms, capacities and a varied culinary offerat the same time, it is an ideal venue for various events and the perfect hotel for a relaxing getaway.

Proud holders of green certificates

  • GREEN KEY – the Bled Rose hotel facility meets all the necessary criteria for obtaining the Green Key sustainability label. By obtaining the Green Key sustainability sign, we are co-creating a green story of sustainable tourism.
  • GREEN CUISINE – in the field of gastronomy, we follow the principles of sustainability through the “from the garden to the table” approach, with an emphasis on seasonal, local and natural, and we follow all the guidelines of the green cuisine certificate.
  • GREEN ACCOMMODATION – The Bled Rose hotel has been awarded the Slovenia Green Accommodation sign for its sustainable and green efforts as an accommodation provider.
  • GREEN & SAFE – Bled Rose hotel has adopted all standards for ensuring a safe and healthy stay. As a result, in the procedure for obtaining the “Safe and Healthy at Events” Certificate, he proved that he meets the criteria of the Congress Office of Slovenia and thus received the “Safe and Healthy at Events” Certificate in two categories: “Safe venue and Safe event organizer.
